Merry Puppins!



Meet Merry Puppins - A Sitter with a Spoonfull of Sugar!

"I have loved and learned from dogs my whole life. My family always had a pooch in the backyard and I developed a unique affinity for our furry friends since I was as young as I can rememer. When I finally "grew up" and had my own place in college, I started to foster dogs. I learned about the app/website Rover from my sister. I have been on Rover for six years."

"I currently live in Little Rockey Run, the neighborhood across from Centreville High School. I have two pups of my own. Sophie is an 8 year-old Greater Swiss Mountain Dog. Friendly and playful, Sophie is patient and motherly towards puppies. Penny is my first puppy of my own, and she is teaching me new things every day. Penny is a curious 6 month-old Pomeranian/Toy Poodle mix. Between our two dogs and my boyfriend, there will always be someone to entertain your dog!"
